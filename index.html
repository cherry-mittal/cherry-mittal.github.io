My Journey

Engineer by training.
AI/ML specialist by passion.

With 6+ years building software and 4 years deep in production AI/ML, I sit at the intersection of cutting-edge research and real-world delivery. My journey into AI was ignited by a simple question: what happens when language models actually ship?

At Optum, I found the answer. I engineered CareVoice — a real-time call intelligence system using Whisper STT + Llama-3 — cutting post-call documentation time by 40% for healthcare agents handling thousands of calls a day. That's AI doing real work in a high-stakes domain.

Today I architect LLM-powered systems from first principles: fine-tuning models with LoRA/PEFT, building Text-to-SQL engines with LangChain, and shipping multimodal vision pipelines to production. I don't just experiment — I deliver.
